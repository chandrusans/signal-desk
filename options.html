Not investment advice — read this before trading options

Single-leg long calls and puts can lose 100% of premium if the underlying doesn't move as expected before expiration. The recommendation engine ranks contracts by underlying setup + strike + expiry + liquidity + bid-ask spread — it does not know your risk tolerance, account size, or tax situation. Delta is an estimate from moneyness, not Black-Scholes. Exit target underlying-prices assume near-expiry mechanics with no theta modelling. Always verify quotes in your broker before submitting an order, use limit orders (never market orders on options), and size positions so the max loss is one you're prepared to take.
